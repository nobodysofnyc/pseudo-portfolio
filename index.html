Redeeming a gift

A fullscreen experience for a gift recipient to choose which gift from their printed book that they want delievered. They choose their own address as the last step.

iPad-CMS.mov

Publishing Wantful stories via backend CMS, which distributes content to the iPad app and to the web.

Nordstrom Gift Service – Video

Partnership iPad web app experience for implimentation in Nordstrom retail stores. We designed and built the app to be quickly updated and iterated upon. The two of us were tasked with framing out user stories and putting together the experience from first sight of the iPads in the store through to checkout.

Nordstrom Gift Service – Prototype

This is a tappable prototype we used to quickly iterate and get feedback from the Nordstrom team.

MIDI Keyboard

Notifications.js

Notifications.js was designed as an extremely lightweight abstraction layer between JavaScript events and the DOM. An attempt at bringing NSNotificationCenter style communication to the web.
